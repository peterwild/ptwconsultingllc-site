Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how PTW Consulting LLC ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with our private QuickBooks Online integration. This Application is not available to the public and does not collect data from end users.

2. Information We Access

This Application accesses QuickBooks Online data — specifically customer records and invoices — solely to create and send property management invoices. This access is limited to the single QBO company owned and operated by PTW Consulting LLC.

3. How We Use Information

Data accessed through QuickBooks Online is used only to generate invoices. It is not stored, shared, sold, or used for any other purpose.

4. Data Storage

OAuth tokens required to authenticate with QuickBooks Online are stored locally on the operator's machine and are not transmitted to any third-party server.

5. Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties. This Application communicates only with Intuit's QuickBooks Online API.
